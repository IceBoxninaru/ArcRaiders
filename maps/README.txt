Place your map images here so they load as defaults in the app.

Expected filenames (all .jpg):
- dam.jpg
- spaceport.jpg
- bluegate.jpg
- buried.jpg
- stella_upper.jpg  (Stella Montis 上層)
- stella_lower.jpg  (Stella Montis 下層)

If these files are missing, the app will fall back to the grid background and you can still upload a local image from the UI.
